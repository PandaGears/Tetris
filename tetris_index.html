So... It's exactly as you think, this is a Tetris Game


The Rules for it is as follows:

Controls: use the LEFT or RIGHT directional keys to shift left or right
to speed up the the falling pieces, press the DOWN directional key
to rotate the pieces, press the UP directional key

Pick a track below to choose which song you want to hear in the background

(for no song, if you just opened the game on a new tab now, you can just skip to Go)

Once picked, click on the Go button to go to the game
